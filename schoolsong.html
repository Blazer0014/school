SSHS School Song

We built our school on thee o lord to thee we bring our common need; the loving heart,

The helpful word the helpful word the tender thoughts to kindly deeds.

We work together in thy sight we live together in thy love;

Guide thou are faltering steps aright,

And lift our thoughts to heaven above.

Hold thou each hand to keep it just touch thou are lips and make them pure;

If thou art with us lord we must be faithful friends and comrades sure.

We changed but thou art still the same; the same good master teacher, friend;

We changed but Lord we bear thy name,

To journey with it to the end.

Amen.


Fabian, Aljeur Emmanuel 8-Green
